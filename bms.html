The Balanced Man Scholarship

Request an Application

Welcome to the Balanced Man Scholarship (BMS) Headquarters.  The BMS is our way of rewarding community members for their embodiment of the values the National Sigma Phi Epsilon Fraternity upholds: Scholarship, Leadership, Athletics, Chivalry/Community Service. It is through these endeavors can a balanced life be lived.

There will be one scholarship awarded in the amount of $500 after an application and interview process.

Requirements for application are the following:

  • Applicant Achieved Levels of Excellence in Scholarship, Leadership, Athletics, and Community Service.

  • Applicant Status is a Graduating High School Senior or a Student Entering their Sophomore Year in College.

  • Applicant is a Male Who Will Attend the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor in the Fall 2000 and is Currently NOT a Member of a Fraternity.

  • Applicant does NOT need to join or attempt to gain membership into Sigma Phi Epsilon.

The deadline for requesting applications via webpage submission is May 8, 2000.  If you feel you may miss this deadline or if there are any questions regarding the scholarship, please contact Gerald Mangona, Balanced Man Scholarship Chair at (734) 623-0613 or e-mail gmangona@umich.edu.
