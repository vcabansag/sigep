Dear Michigan Alpha Alumni,

On the second anniversary of Michigan Alpha’s recolonization at the University of Michigan, we are proud to announce our chapter’s rechartering this past Fall. Michigan Alpha was rechartered on Friday, October 15th, 1999, at our black tie formal in Ann Arbor hosted by the Sheraton Inn. We thank all of you the area and Michigan Alpha alumni that helped us celebrate our new beginning.

We are a growing chapter. To date our chapter is eighty men strong with a maximum capacity of eighteen residents in the chapter house. Our house on 725 Oxford, while small for our chapter, is a temporary location for us; we are pursuing a larger house for Fall 2000. 

There is much to celebrate this year. Come join us for our formal charter installation on October 15th. I look forward to seeing you there.
