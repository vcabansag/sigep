Sigma Phi Epsilon is the only fraternity at the University of Michigan that is NO HAZING and NO PLEDGING.  Our alternative to the traditional fraternity is through the Balanced Man Program which acclimates new members into SigEp throughout their college experience. Sigma Phi Epsilon strives to excel in areas of scholarship, leadership, athletics, and being gentlemen.  Come see what we're about.
